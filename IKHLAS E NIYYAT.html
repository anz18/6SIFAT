IKHLAS E NIYYAT

Ehmiyat

Hum jo bhi amal karte hai sahin hai ya ghalat yeh ulma e akram hi bata sakte hai . ikhlas k afaesla Allah hi kareng euar wo uss waqt karenge jis waqt hamara amal karne ka waqt nikal chuk ahoga wo hog a qayamat ka din.

Maqsad

Hamare andar la ehmiyat ka jazba ajae. Hum jo bhi naek amal karein khalis Allah ke liye karein

Jabair ibne-Abdullah R.a narrates that Rasulullah saws said: The poor among the Muslims will enter Paradise forty years before the rich.(Tirmidhi)

Fazeelat

Ikhlaas walon k liye khushhali hai ke wo hidayat ke chiragh hai . inki wajah se dunya ke saqt se saqt fitne dab jatein hai

  1. Taaleem ke halqon me aur bayanat ki majlis me baith ker iman ki fazeelat ko khoob sunein .
  2. Daawat me chal phir ker Allah ke bandon ki badhayi aur yaqeen ko qoob dawat dein.
  3. Apni zaat se yeh mashq karein k jis jagah maqluq se hota hua dekhlein toh dil aur zaban se yeh iqrar Karen k sab kuch qaliq ne kia aur Allah ki banayi hui maqluq per ghaur o fikr karein.
  4. Dua: duaon k zariye iman ki salamati aur baqi rehne ke liye faqeer aur haqeer se haqeer ban kar Allah se ro ro kar iman ki haqeeqat k liye dua maange.
